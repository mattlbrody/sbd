Get to know Judy VanArsdale

Managing Partner of 3rivers, Judy VanArsdale, began her career at McKesson where she quickly moved from merchandiser, to sales, to business development manager, to sales manager, to VP of Sales (retail), and VP of Sales (National Accounts). She was even “Sales Rep of the Year” once and “VP of the Year” during her career. During that time, she completed her Six Sigma Black Belt training and today uses many of those skills in her Chicago-based wealth management firm, Lakeview Wealth Management.

Today Judy is in the top 1% at LPL Financial having grown her book from $8M in AUM to $350M in just 15 years. Judy’s success has earned her a role on the Advisor Council and the Government Relations Advisor Board (serving as Board President).

Judy was named as one of Forbes "Best in State" for 2020, Forbes "Top Women Advisors" for 2020 and has been an advocate and voice on behalf of the industry for many years. She was a panelist at Barron’s Women’s Conference in 2016, was interviewed as one of only three advisors from LPL’s first Investor and Analyst Day in 2017, and was honored to testify in front of the Ways and Means Subcommittee about DOL Best interest. She has also presented and been a panelist at many LPL conferences and meetings.

Proudest moment as a mentor:
"Watching my team present at Focus 2019 with 450 attendees!"

How did you get where you are today?
"I have always worked hard at everything I have attempted. That is the easy part, having passion for what I do and caring about the people I work with and serve is how I have gotten here today. In my opinion, when you treat those around you with compassion (even if it is having the tough conversations) building a mutual trust is how we grow as people. Everything grows from there."

Why are you three here together?
“When I am with Lisa and Nancy the world just lights up. These two women inspire me to be better every day. Just when I think maybe I should start to plan retirement they challenge me to be a part of something better than each of us. 3rivers enables me to help the next generation of advisors become the best they can be. To share the wisdom and knowledge - and more importantly - the mistakes. We laugh, we cry, we care and together the three of us have found a way to share. We learn every day and are so grateful for what we have. Together we feel a confidence and power that individually I don't think I have."
