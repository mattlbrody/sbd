Frequently Asked Questions

How is this training any different from what I already know?

If you are like any of the thousands of advisors we’ve consulted over the years you’ve been trained (and trained well) in finance and sales…but not business management. 3rivers is passionate about helping advisors learn how to run a business – not just a practice. Our entire training emphasis is oriented around helping you to run a team, managing your finances and improve the operational efficiency of your business so you can run it like a CEO.

What makes you qualified to help me?

It’s all about who. The leaders of our team have over 50 years combined experience, are award winning advisors within LPL Financial's community of advisors, and are consistently booked for main-stage speaking slots at top events. They are widely regarded as leaders and advocates in the industry.

How does the training work? Is it a course?

We recognize you have unique needs and are coming to us with different goals, time constraints, and levels of experience. Therefore, our training is not a traditional course. Instead the content is provided in an open architecture format that allows you to pursue your own needs – watch what you want, when you want, as many times as you need. Each module contains a tool of some kind, a checklist, a form, an example, and then a training video to show you how to use it.

Why Are You Three Doing This?

We love what we’re doing today and will continue to serve our clients. But we also have wisdom about running a business that we want to share with the industry. 3rivers gives us a simple way to share those best practices with more people, easier and faster, in an organized way.
